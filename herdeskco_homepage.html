Our Story

Built by a woman who saw
the gap firsthand.

HDC
Women
Owned

Her Desk Co. was founded on a simple observation: the most overwhelmed person in a growing aesthetic clinic is almost always the owner.

They're performing treatments, managing staff, marketing their business — and somewhere in between, trying to keep up with everything the back office demands.

We are a women-owned remote administrative support agency built specifically for medical aesthetic clinics and medspas. We build systems, document processes, and become the back office your clinic needs to grow.

We work with a small number of clinics at a time — intentionally — so every client gets real attention and real results.
